About Us

Founded in 2012 by Leonard Reed and Leanne Reed, Imagine-it, is a small organization of talented, driven individuals that focuses on custom web application design and development services. Located in Denver, Colorado we see ourselves as part of a small community. Likewise, we view our clients as partners and our goals have been to grow lasting relationships with them that will stand the test of time.

Although somewhat new to the market, our two principals have nearly 20 years of combined experience in the field. Believing that good craftsmenship and critical thinking are critical to producing and delivering quality products to our customers we strive to make sure everything is just right.

Our Vision:

To provide cutting edge solutions that at an affordable prices to small and medium sized businesses.

Why do we limit our business to small and medium sized businesses you ask?
Because, after working for years with the "big guys", It was all too clear that the small to medium businesses were simply being ignored. Too many are forced into auto generated sites run by fly by night entrapenures. We are dedicated to bringing tier 1 technologies including HTML5, CSS3, CMS and other high end technologies to the masses.
