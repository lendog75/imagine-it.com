One of the most appealing aspects of responsive web design is that a responsive website can provide a
great user-experience across many devices and screen sizes. This is an important characteristic, since
it is impossible to anticipate all the devices and screen sizes searchers will use to access your site.
A site that works well regardless of these variables will provide a better and more consistent
user-experience than a separate mobile site that is designed for a specific device and screen size.
