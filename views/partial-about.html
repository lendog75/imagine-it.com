About Us

Imagine-It is a small but capable Internet consulting company, located in Denver, Colorado. We provide many Internet-related services, including web design, application development and digital branding.

After working for years with the "big guys", including the University of Central Florida (A Top 5 Research University) and Johns Hopkins, It became all too clear that America's small businesses were not able to take advantage of today's best technologies. We are here to tell everyone, those days are over! Founded in 2012 by Lenny Reed and Leanne Reed, our small organization of talented, driven individuals focused on custom web application design and development services. Located in Denver, Colorado we see ourselves as part of the community. Likewise, we view our clients as partners and our goals have been to grow lasting relationships with them that will stand the test of time.

Although somewhat new to the market, our principals have 20 years of combined experience in the field. Believing that good craftsmenship and critical thinking are critical to producing and delivering quality products to our customers we strive to make sure everything is just right.

Our Mission:

"To provide cutting edge solutions that at affordable prices to small and medium sized businesses."
